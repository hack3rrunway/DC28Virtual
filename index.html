Welcome to the 2020 Digital H@ck3r Runw@y landing page.

For the second year, H@ck3r Runw@y is bringing together hacker trend setters. Make it SMART, LIGHT it up, OBFUSCATE something, or be GEEKY on fleek. Enter clothing, shoes, jewelry, hats or accessories. If you wear it, the runway can handle it. Anyone can enter, but honest and only submit something YOU created. The entry form allows you to link to blog or video of proof of your work. Since this is virtual, I want to keep it simple for folks.

FILL In the FORM and cast your vote for favorites.

Selected judges will decide on the winners for the categories below, but your vote will select the People's Choice winner.

SIGNUP START: July 8, 2020

SIGNUP DEADLINE: August 7, 2020

Awards will be handed out in 5 categories and voters will select a final People's Choice winner:

  • New categories for the competition
  • Digital wearable - LED, electronic, non-interactive
  • Smart wearable - interactive, sound reactive, light reactive, temperature sensitive
  • Nerd wearable - passive design, geek fabric, 3d printed, cool as heck
  • Covid-19 wearable (replacing Live) - did you bling out your mask and/or shield
  • Miscellaneous wearable - obfuscated, lock picks jewelry, shims, card skimmers

Winners will be selected based on, but no limited to:

  • Uniqueness
  • Trendy
  • Practical
  • Couture
  • Creativity
  • Relevance
  • Originality
  • Presentation
  • Mastery

Stay up to date with changes by following @hack3runway on Twitter. Don't have an entry? You can still participate. Stop by and vote for your favorites.

Your images and/or video will be used in a public presentation for the Defcon 28 Conference. Thank you for helping to make this a success.
